5.6.4.12.2.1 Result
The method returns the data input that has been consumed in the hash as the method result.
Results of the hashing operation are stored in the BufferOut cellblock of the HashInit method, if that
parameter is included.  Otherwise the results are stored in the invoking hash object’s Accumulator
column.  If the BufferOut parameter is specified in the HashInit method, the Hash method Result
SHALL be empty.
