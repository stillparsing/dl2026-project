5.6.3.3.6 Signer
This is a uidref to the signing/verification credential object.  This is the signing credential whose public
key or symmetric key decrypts the proof to reveal the proof’s underlying hash when the Verify method
is invoked; or whose private or symmetric key encrypts the proof when the Sign method is invoked on
this credential; or whose HMAC key is used when the HMAC methods are invoked on this credential.
