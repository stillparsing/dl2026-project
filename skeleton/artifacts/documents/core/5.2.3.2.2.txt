5.2.3.2.2 SPSessionID
The SPSessionID parameter in the SyncSession invocation is the TPer side session number, which is
assigned by the TPer. All further invocations in this series of method invocations and responses use
this TPer-assigned session number in the SPSessionID parameter.
This is the number that becomes the TSN portion of the packet header Session field (see 3.2.3.3 and
3.3.7.1).
