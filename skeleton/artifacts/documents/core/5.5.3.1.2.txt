5.5.3.1.2 HaveHigh
If the value of this column is True, then the values in the High Trust time columns (HighByWhom,
HighSetTime, HighInitialTimer, and HighLag) are meaningful.  If the value of the TrustMode column
is Low or Timer then the value of this column SHALL be False.
This column SHALL NOT be modifiable by the host.
