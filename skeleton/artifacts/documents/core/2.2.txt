2.2 Architecture Components
Begin Informative Content
The architecture is illustrated in Figure 1, which shows a single Multicomponent Trusted Platform
(MCTP) with one Trusted Peripheral (TPer).  An MCTP supports 1 or more TPers.  Figure 1 shows just
one example.  Other possibilities include multiple hosts communicating with a single Storage
Device/TPer, a single host communicating with multiple Storage Devices/TPers, etc.
