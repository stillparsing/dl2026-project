5.4.3.1 IssueSP (SP Method)
This method is used to issue SPs on those TPers where the SPs are not fixed by the manufacturer.
ORGs that are permitted to only issue certain Templates into new SPs are controlled by attributes in the
ORG’s certificate.
ThisSP.IssueSP [

SPName : name,

Size : uinteger,

Templates : list [ TemplateObjectUID ... ],

AdminExch : key_size,

Enabled : boolean ]
=>
[ UID : uid, Size : uinteger ]
