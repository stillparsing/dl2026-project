4.4 Default Authorities
The initial authorities defined in this specification that MAY affect the life cycle states are defined for:
a. Base Template (Table 209) – the Admins Authority (SP owner) and Makers Authority.
b. Admin Template (Table 216) – In addition to the Base Template Authorities, the Issuing
(and related) authorities, and the SID (TPer Owner) authority.
These are the only authorities that are within the scope of the specification.  Additional authorities MAY
be defined in a Security Subsystem Class; during SP personalization and operational use, as required
and permitted by the access control settings defined here; or both.
