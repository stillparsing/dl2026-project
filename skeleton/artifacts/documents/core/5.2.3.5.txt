5.2.3.5 CloseSession Method
This method SHALL only be transmitted by the TPer.  The TPer MAY transmit this method to notify the
host that it is aborting the session identified in the CloseSession method, as well as all open un-
committed transactions and methods undergoing processing (see 3.3.7.1.5).
SMUID.CloseSession [
RemoteSessionNumber : uinteger,
LocalSessionNumber : uinteger ]
