5.6.4.15.2.1 Result
The method returns the DataInput that has been consumed in the HMAC operation as the method
result.
Results of the operation are stored in the BufferOut cellblock of the HMACInit method, if that parameter
is included.  Otherwise the results are stored in the invoking hash object’s Accumulator column.  If the
BufferOut parameter is specified in the HMACInit method, the HMAC method Result SHALL be empty.
