5.2.2.4.1.2 MaxPacketSize
The host SHOULD NOT send a Packet whose size (including Packet header) exceeds the value of the
TPer’s MaxPacketSize property. If the host sends a Packet that is too large, the TPer SHALL abort the
session associated with the Packet. In the case of a Packet that is too large on the Control Session, the
TPer SHALL discard and ignore the Packet.
The TPer SHALL NOT send a Packet whose size (including Packet header) exceeds the value of the
host’s MaxPacketSize property.
