3.2.3.5.2.2.3 Pad
This field contains any necessary padding required to fulfill the alignment constraints for the encryption
mode in use.  For AES-CBC encryption, the length of the Pad field SHALL include a number of padding
bytes such that the total length of the Data field plus the Pad field is congruent to zero mod 16.  For
GCM and CCM, there is no required padding.
The value for pad bytes SHALL be 0x00.
