5.3.4.1.2.5 AdminExch
The AdminExch authority represents the initial credential value submitted during issuance.  This is the
authority that enables the host to open a secure, implicitly authenticated session to the host’s SP and
personalize that SP.  In the case of the Authority table in the Admin SP, the Base Template Authority
AdminExch SHALL be disabled.  At issuance, prior to personalization, the AdminExch authority has a
RespExch column value set to the AdminExch authority's UID.
