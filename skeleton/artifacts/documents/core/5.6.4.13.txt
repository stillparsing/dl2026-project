5.6.4.13 Hash Method Group – HashFinalize (Object Method)
Invocation of the HashFinalize method causes the TPer to flush the remaining, non-blocked data
through the hash and sets the BufferOut cellblock specified in the HashInit method.  If the BufferOut
cellblock was not supplied to the HashInit method, the hash result is set to the Accumulator column of
the invoking hash object.
If there is no open hash “stream” for the invoking hash object, the method invocation SHALL fail.
