3.3.4.7 Communication Layer Commands
For any given ComID, the host is expected to issue HANDLE_COMID_REQUEST and
GET_COMID_RESPONSE commands in pairs. Consecutive GET_COMID_RESPONSE commands
SHALL return data corresponding to the last HANDLE_COMID_REQEUST received by the TPer. The
response MAY be regenerated by the TPer at the time of receipt of the command.
