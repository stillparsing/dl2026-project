3.4.1.1 Admin SP
Begin Informative Content
The Admin SP maintains information about other SPs and the TPer as a whole and enables creation of
other SPs under issuance control.
End Informative Content
There SHALL be exactly one Admin SP on every TPer that has SPs or that is able to have SPs issued.
If present, the Admin SP SHALL NOT be able to be deleted, disabled, or frozen.  The Admin SP SHALL
have the name "Admin."
