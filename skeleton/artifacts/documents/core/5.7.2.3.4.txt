5.7.2.3.4 Key
This column stores the key associated with this K_AES_128 object. Non-tweakable cipher modes such
as ECB, CBC, CFB, OFB, GCM, CCM, and CTR SHALL use the 16-byte option for the key size.
Tweakable cipher modes such as XTS or LRW SHALL use the 32-byte option
For MediaEncryption mode, the content of the Key column MAY be vendor-specific.
