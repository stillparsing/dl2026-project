5.2.3.1.1 HostSessionID
The HostSessionID parameter in the StartSession invocation is the host-side session number
assigned and used by the host to identify this session.  All further invocations in this series of method
invocations and responses use this host-assigned session number in the HostSessionID parameter.
This is the number that becomes the HSN portion of the packet header Session field (see 3.2.3.3 and
3.3.7.1).
