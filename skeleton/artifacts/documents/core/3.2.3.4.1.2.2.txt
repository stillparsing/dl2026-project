3.2.3.4.1.2.2 Pad
The pad field ensures that the boundaries between subpackets (and therefore packets) are aligned to
4-byte boundaries.  The number of pad bytes SHALL be (-Subpacket.Length modulo 4).  This field
SHALL be zeroes.
Begin Informative Content
The receiver of a Subpacket is able to unambiguously determine how many bytes of real data there are
by examining the Length field in the Subpacket header.
Note that the standard C library does not handle the modulo operation with negative numbers properly.
End Informative Content
